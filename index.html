Why come to Anza Borrego?

Pristine night skies

Anza-Borrego’s status as an International Dark Sky Park signifies its exceptional night sky quality, providing a pristine canvas for observing the cosmos.

Ideal conditions for astrophotography

The dry desert air and stable atmospheric conditions enhance visibility and are perfect for capturing sharp, clear astrophotographs.

Unique experiences

Its open skies offer a front-row seat to spectacular celestial events, including meteor showers, planetary alignments, and eclipses.
